# ENCUMBRANCE

> Encumbrance is a measure of both the weight and bulk a character is carrying. The character's movement rate is based on the total weight of all significant items carried, including treasure, weapons, and armor.

Encumbrance is measured in Coins. Ten coins are equivalent to one pound. 

The weight of coins and other treasure carried is directly tracked. The weight of the character's armor and weapons is also tracked - the weight of these items is noted in the equipment database. Random miscellaneous adventuring gear such as backpacks, spikes, sacks and so forth can be considered as a total of 80 coins of weight.

## MOVEMENT

The character's movement rate is based on the total weight of all significant items carried.

| Encumbrance      | Movement Rate Outdoors (in combat) |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Up to 400 coins  | 120' (40')                         |
| Up to 600 coins  | 90' (30')                          |
| Up to 800 coins  | 60' (20')                          |
| Up to 1600 coins | 30' (10')                          |

The maximum load any character can carry is 1600 coins of weight.
