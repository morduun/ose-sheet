# MERCENARIES

> Hired soldiers who will guard, patrol, and otherwise serve in wilderness settings, but only as part of a larger force, not an adventuring party. Mercenaries do not accompany characters on adventures. 
>
> Note that as mercenaries are hired by a character to perform specific services, they are not treated as retainers and thus do not count towards a character's maximum number of retainers - but they _will_ drain the character's gold monthly!

## Wages

- Outside of Wartime: as listed
- During Wartime: doubled

## Upkeep

Monthly rate of pay includes food and basic gear. Most mercenaries already have weaponss and armor when hired. Armorers (a specialist) are required for upkeep on mercenaries' weapons and armo.

## Details

### Archer

Armed with shortbow, leather armor, dagger, and shield.

### Archer, Mounted

Mounted on a riding horse. Equipped with a shortbow and a dagger.

### Crossbowman

Armed with crossbow and chainmail.

### Crossbow, Mounted

Mounted on a mule. Equipped with a crossbow and a horseman's hammer.

### Footman, Light

Equipped with sword, leather armor, and shield

### Footman, Heavy

Equipped with sword, chainmail, and shield

### Horseman, Light

Mounted on a riding horse. Equipped with a lance, a short sword, and leather armor.

### Horseman, Medium

Mounted on a light warhorse. Equipped with a lance, an arming sword, and chainmail.

### Horseman, Heavy

Mounted on a heavy warhorse. Equipped with a lance, an arming sword, and plate mail.

### Longbowman

Armed with a longbow, a sword, and chainmail.

### Peasant

Armed with a staff.

### Wolf Rider

Mounted on a wolf. Equipped with a spear and leather armor.
